About Gail

Agile and adaptive with a focus on user-first mindset and continuous product improvement, Gail is aspiring to work with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to deliver value as a Product Manager. She is enthusiastic about the intersection of UI/UX design, engineering and business.

As a quick learner of new areas, she adapts easily to changing requirements, priorities and schedules. Gail's education and experience in various sectors (web development, small business hospitality, biotech, and legal) have given her the ability to communicate with a diverse range of subject-matter experts and users to deliver a clear vision and product outcome. Her easy-going nature coupled with her detailed organization make her a central asset on any team.

Let Gail ease cross-team collaboration in the development of your product while keeping users' needs and ROI in mind. Let's chat!
